Key Projects

1. Mouth Open-Close Detection Using Python & OpenCV

Developed a real-time mouth gesture detection system using **Python** and **OpenCV**, designed to assist individuals with speech impairments or limited mobility. The system detects mouth movements and translates them into commands for interaction with electronic devices, showcasing the potential of **computer vision** and **machine learning** in accessibility tools.

2. Tongue Operated Wheelchair (Funded by Government of India)

Created a **tongue-operated wheelchair** for users with severe disabilities, utilizing **IoT**-enabled sensors and embedded systems. The project empowers individuals to control their wheelchair through tongue movements, improving their mobility. The initiative was funded by the **Government of India** as part of their efforts to promote assistive technology.

3. Travel Website (Full Stack Development)

Designed and developed a **full-stack travel website** that offers a seamless experience for users to browse destinations, book travel packages, and manage bookings. Built with **HTML**, **CSS**, **JavaScript**, and **PHP**, and powered by **MySQL** for data storage. The project also incorporates responsive design principles to ensure a smooth experience on mobile devices.

4. Drone-Based Surveillance System for Vessels in Port Areas

Developed a **drone-based surveillance system** designed to monitor vessels in port areas and detect potential encroachments. Using **Python** for data processing and **Flask** for real-time web-based visualizations, the system provides port authorities with valuable insights and improves security in port operations.
